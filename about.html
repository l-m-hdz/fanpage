Born in 1984, Gina Rodriguez is an American actress who got her start appearing on television shows like Law & Order and The Mentalist. She made a big impression in 2012 with the indie musical Filly Brown and made her star turn when she won the leading role of Jane in The CW's Jane the Virgin in 2014. With the success of the show and a Golden Globe for Best Actress under her belt (which she won in 2015), Rodriguez has been taking on big-budget film projects such as Deepwater Horizon (2016) and Annihilation (2018).
